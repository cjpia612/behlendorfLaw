Welcome to the Law Offices of Theodore A. Behlendorf

Who Are We?

We are an immigration law firm based in Los Angeles. Our focus is exclusively on handling immigration cases, representing both individual and corporate clients within and outside the United States. Theodore Behlendorf and Maria Sedano each have more than 20 years of experience practicing all aspects of immigration law.

Click to Meet the Team
Our Scope of Work

Our services include assisting clients in obtaining nonimmigrant visas and permanent resident (green card) status for themselves, their families, and their employees. The Law Offices represent clients from all over the United States and the world. We are also fluent in the following languages:

  • English
  • Tagalog
  • Cebuano
  • Spanish
  • Mandarin
  • Russian
