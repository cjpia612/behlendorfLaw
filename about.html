About the Lawyers

Our Law Firm

Theodore A. Behlendorf⋮
Bioclose

Theodore Behlendorf is a graduate of Tulane Law School and has been a member of the California State Bar since 1995. His practice focuses on Immigration and Nationality law, with an emphasis on immigrant and non-immigrant employment and family-based petitions. Atty. Behlendorf specializes in cases before the Immigration Courts, Board of Immigration Appeals, and Appellate Courts.

Maria R. Sedano ⋮
Bioclose

Maria "Joy" Sedano is an associate at the Law Offices of Theodore Behlendorf, where she represents individual and corporate clients in their family- and employment-based petitions and other stand-alone applications before the U.S. Citizenship & Immigration Services. She received her law degree from Loyola Law School and her Bachelor of Science degree, magna cum laude, in Business Administration with an emphasis in Accounting from Mount St. Mary's University.

History of the Firm ⋮
The Firmclose We are a Los Angeles-based law firm focused solely on handling immigration cases, representing both individual and corporate clients within and outside the United States. Our services include assisting clients in obtaining nonimmigrant visas and permanent resident (green card) status for themselves, their families, or for their employees. Theodore (Ted) Behlendorf has more than 25 years of experience practicing immigration law, including removal defense. Maria (Joy) Sedano also has 20 years of experience working exclusively in all aspects of the immigration process, five of which as a practicing attorney. Immigration is all we do.
