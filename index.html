About Us

We are Theodore A. Behlendorf an Immigration & Naturalization firm based in Los Angeles. Our focused exclusively on handling immigration cases, representing both individual and corporate clients within and outside the United States. Theodore Behlendorf and Maria Sedano have more than 20 years of experience practicing all aspects of immigration law.

Scope of Work

Our services include assisting clients in obtaining nonimmigrant visas and permanent resident (green card) status for themselves, their families, or for their employees. The Law Offices resprents clients from all over the United States and the world.

  • NON IMMIGRANT - TEMPORARY STATUS
  • B, E, F, H1B, H2A, H2B, I, J, K, L, M, O, P, Q, R, TN, Dependent, and Others Categories

  • NON IMMIGRANT - PERMANENT STATUS - FAMILY BASED
  • First, Second, Third, and Fourth Preference

  • IMMIGRANT - PERMANENT STATUS - EMPLOYMENT BASED
  • EB1, EB2, EB3, EB4, EB5

  • GREEN CARD LOTTERY, ASYLUM/REFUGEE, CITIZENSHIP, CONSULAR PRACTICE, AND OTHERS
